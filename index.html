20230829 C++ vs. Fortran Round 1

Ok , I have been done some testing comparing C++ and Fotran90 Smoothed particle Hydrodynamics codes. I get less than the half of calculation time Fotran 90. I discovered later that the comparison has been not fear, due to one of the 12 threads was writing outputs each 1/10th of the total time, whereas F90 code only writes an output at end time. C++ is with an AOS scheme (vector of class Particle Pointers) and F90 is with a SOA scheme (Structure of arrays). #programming #coding #simulation #computational #solidmechanics
