Computational Mechanics

OpenSource Mechanics is a technical site dedicated to the study and development of numerical methods for large deformation simulation.

Topics covered include:

  • Finite Element Methods
  • Large deformation simulation
  • Metal forming processes
  • GPU accelerated solvers
  • Explicit and implicit formulations

Projects

  • Explicit FEM solver (CPU / GPU)
  • Implicit viscoplastic forming solver
  • Remeshing techniques for forming simulation
