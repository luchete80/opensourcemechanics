WeldFormSPH

SPH Solid Mechanics Large Deformation Metal Forming

WeldFormSPH is the particle-based branch of the toolset, focused on severe-deformation mechanics where mesh distortion becomes a central limitation for standard Lagrangian FEM.

Current focus

  • Elasto-plastic solid mechanics
  • Contact handling for forming processes
  • Material laws such as Hollomon, Johnson-Cook, and bilinear models
  • Benchmarking SPH against FEM and commercial references
