Validation Case: Closed Die Forging

Metal Forming Closed Die Forging Die Filling Validation Case

This page is reserved for a closed-die forging benchmark aimed at checking cavity filling, flash formation, and solver robustness under strong contact confinement and large local strain.

What this benchmark checks

  • Filling progression inside the die cavity
  • Flash development and boundary flow
  • Load peaks related to die closure
  • Strain localization near corners and thin gaps
