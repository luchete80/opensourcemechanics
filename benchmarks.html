Validation Cases

Benchmarks and validation cases sit at the same strategic level as videos: not because they drive more traffic today, but because they become durable technical assets.

One well-documented benchmark can remain useful for years through citations, technical searches, GitHub, LinkedIn, and direct comparison workflows. For that reason, this section is organized as one main landing page with categories inside it.
