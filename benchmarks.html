Benchmarks and Validation Cases

This section is organized by specific validation case rather than by method family. Each page focuses on one benchmark, the reference problem setup, and the comparison targets used to evaluate the solver behavior.

That makes it easier to compare formulations across FEM, SPH, or mixed approaches without hiding the actual forming process behind a broad category label.
